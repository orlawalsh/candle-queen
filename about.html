About Us

Candle Queen is an Irish owned business. Operating in the south east of Ireland. All candles are handmade and 100% soy based. We have a range of signature blends but custom blends are available on request. So if you have an idea in mind feel free to send us a message so we can help bring your ideal candle to life. We also provide a selection of pre-set monthly giftboxes or mix and match giftboxes.
